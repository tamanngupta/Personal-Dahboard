How the Trajectory Score works

The score starts at 50. Every day you do your needle-mover and mark Yes: +3. Every No: −4. Miss logging entirely: −2.

Every goal sub-task you tick: +2. Score is capped between 0–100.

Under 30 = full send on something drastic. 30–60 = you're trying, now try harder. 60–80 = don't coast. 80+ = stay paranoid, that's when you get complacent.
